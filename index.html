The Defence of Jola Prime

This is a 2D platformer game based on a galactic defence ship. The Invaders have come and you need to defeat them to save the galaxy. The player character can run, jump and shoot bullets at the cursor's location. These bullets can be used to kill enemies and also solve certain platforming challenges. The inspiration for this game came from other 2D platformers in the industry such as: Hollow Knight, Shovel Knight and Spelunky. These games combined enjoyable platforming with difficult enemies and nice level design.

Features:

  • Multiple level scenes: Hub, level1, boss1
  • Shooting feature that interacts with the world (kills enemies, triggers buttons)
  • Sound effects, Music
  • Fun platforming and engaging boss

Controls

Default movement controls are the left, up and right arrow keys. Once in level 1, you can shoot with the left mouse button.
